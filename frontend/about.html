The Problem

The Recruitment Challenge

Hiring pipelines drown in volume long before they reach the right people — and keyword filters quietly throw the best candidates away.

Thousands of CVs

A single open role can attract thousands of applications, far more than any team can read with care.

Manual Screening

Recruiters spend hours scanning resumes by hand, time that scales linearly with every new applicant.

Keyword Matching

Legacy filters reward exact phrasing, not real ability, so qualified people get filtered out by accident.

Missed Talent

The strongest candidates are often described differently than the job post, so they never surface at all.

Our Mission

We believe hiring should reward what people can actually do — not how well their resume happens to be written.

3000-CV-MATCHING was built to replace brittle keyword filters with a semantic understanding of skills, context and intent — so every candidate is read fairly, at any scale.

Fair by design

01 · Client Layer

Recruiters upload resumes and define role requirements through a responsive browser interface — no installs required.

02 · FastAPI Backend

A lightweight async API orchestrates uploads, parsing jobs, and result delivery with predictable, low-latency responses.

03 · AI Engine

Sentence-transformer models convert resumes and job descriptions into vectors, then score candidates by semantic similarity.

04 · Storage

Parsed documents, embeddings, and generated reports are persisted for fast retrieval and auditability.
